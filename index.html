    The activity levels required by your dog depends on its breed. For example, low energy dogs such as giant breed dogs (e.g: Great Dane) or brachycephalic (e.g: French Bulldog), naturally require less activity than high energy dogs such as herding breed dogs (e.g: Border Collie). So, if you have a Border Collie that gets 30 minutes of exercise a day, this would be considered light activity level. But 30 minutes of activity for a French Bulldog would be considered medium!
    Consider your dog's breed when selecting activity level!

    General guideline:
    Low activity: 0-60min.
    Medium activity: 60-90min.
    High activity: 90min+.
